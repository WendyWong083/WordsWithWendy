projects

In 2023, my team and I produced a series of features on the rise of artificial intelligence, exploring its impact across different areas and helping audiences understand both its benefits and challenges. These projects highlight AI's growing influence and the ethical, security, and societal questions it raises.

Living with AI: Scams and Security

Investigating how fraudsters exploit AI and deepfake technology, and what can be done to protect against these threats.

Winner of Best Business Technology Reporting at the Hang Seng University Business Journalism Awards 2024.

Learning with AI: Bots vs. Brains

Examining how AI is transforming education and whether it can truly replace human learning.

Recognized at the Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union Prizes 2024, Perspective Awards.

Healing with AI: Data and Diagnostics

Exploring AI's role in revolutionizing healthcare, from medical data analysis to diagnostics.
