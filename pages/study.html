I'm currently pursuing a master's degree in AI and Digital Media at Hong Kong Baptist University to better understand how technology is shaping the media industry.

In my first semester, I learned Python, SQL, data analysis and machine learning through courses taught by Dr Paolo Mengoni. Coming from a non-coding background, it wasn't easy at first, but Dr Mengoni was incredibly supportive. With his help, I kept pushing forward and was thrilled to get first place in performance.

This term I'm building on that foundation with more interesting courses, including:

  • AI for Digital Media Workshop (Dr. Lan Liang) – A hands-on course exploring AI-powered media design, social media dynamics, and product deployment, with a focus on how AI is transforming the media landscape.
  • AI and Digital Media (Dr. Xiaoyi Fu) – An introduction to core AI concepts and techniques, covering logic and agents, search algorithms and neural networks
  • AI for Social Good (Dr. Zhu Yuner) – A critical examination of ethical AI development, addressing key issues like fairness, privacy, copyright, and bias to understand the governance of responsible AI systems.

These courses are equipping me with advanced technical and strategic insights into AI's evolving role in digital media and society.
